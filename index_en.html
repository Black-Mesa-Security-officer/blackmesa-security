About the Division

The Black Mesa Security Division is dedicated to maintaining the safety and integrity of all operations within the Black Mesa Research Facility. Our responsibilities include site-wide surveillance, access control, emergency response, and protection of personnel and classified projects.

Uniform Policy

Security personnel are required to wear designated uniforms appropriate to their duties. The standard attire includes a navy shirt and trousers, a ballistic vest with the Black Mesa insignia, and additional gear such as jackets or helmets depending on the assignment. HEV suits are mandated in high-risk zones.

Equipment Overview

All officers are issued a Glock 17 sidearm, a communication device, and a utility belt. Additional gear such as MP5 SMGs, shotguns, and tactical flashlights may be assigned based on threat level and operational requirements. Use of specialized equipment is restricted to trained personnel only.
